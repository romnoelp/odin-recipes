Strawberry Topping

A strawberry topped cheesecake.

Ingredients

  • 1 pint fresh strawberries
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla (Optional)

Steps

  1. Hull strawberries and cut them in half.
  2. Combine strawberries, sugar, and vanilla in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook, stirring occasionally, until mixture sizzles and juice begins to form.
  3. Continue stirring; mash a few strawberries with a wooden spoon or heat-proof spatula to help produce the syrup. Cook until sauce thickens, about 15 minutes. Remove from the heat.
  4. Transfer 1/3 of the strawberry sauce to a blender. Cover and hold the lid down with a potholder while you puree the mixture.
  5. Add pureed mixture back into the remaining sauce and stir until blended.
  6. Serve over a slice of cheesecake.
